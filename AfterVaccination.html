COVID-19: After You Are Fully Vaccinated


COVID-19 vaccination is effective at protecting you from getting sick. Based on what we know about COVID-19 vaccines, people who have been fully vaccinated can start safely doing many of the things Wisconsinites have missed during the pandemic, like gathering with friends and family. Being fully vaccinated does more than make you feel safe around friends, family, and neighbors. Benefits of getting vaccinated


here are many reasons to get the COVID-19 vaccine.
  • COVID-19 vaccination will help keep you from getting COVID-19.
  • Getting vaccinated can protect people around you.
  • COVID-19 vaccines are safe and effective.
  • Once you are fully vaccinated, you can start doing more.
  • None of the COVID-19 vaccines can make you sick with COVID-19 or make you test positive on a viral test (PCR or antigen).
  • COVID-19 vaccination is an important tool to help stop the pandemic.
  • COVID-19 vaccines available in the U.S. offer protection against most variants of SARS-CoV-2.
  • Vaccination is a safer way to help build protection than getting COVID-19.
